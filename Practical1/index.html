Ever wondered what you can do with your old plastic bottles? Well for one you can turn them into reusable pots for your plants. Grow your own herb garden without making a mess!

STOP! Look at all these plastic waste thrown away into the sea. Why throw them when we can use them for something better? In 2019, Singapore generates 930,000 tonnes of plastic waste and only 4% of it is recycled. Plastic debris is clogging up landfills, blocking drains, polluting waterways and contributing to biodiversity loss. Plastic litter on roadsides and beaches and in other public spaces is an eyesore.
